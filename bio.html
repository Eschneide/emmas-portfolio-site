Biography

Hello, my name is Emma Schneider. I'm passionate graphic design student currently pursuing my degree at SUNY New Paltz. With a keen eye for detail and a love for creativity, I specialize in visual communication and branding design.

Throughout my academic journey, I've honed my skills in Adobe Creative Suite, including Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign, mastering techniques in layout design, typography, and digital illustration.

I'm deeply inspired by modern design trends and strive to create impactful and memorable visual experiences. Whether it's crafting logos, designing marketing materials, or exploring the world of digital art, I'm always eager to push boundaries and innovate in the realm of design.
